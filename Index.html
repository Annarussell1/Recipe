BROWNIES?

WHY BROWNIES?

- Brownies are an easy, fun, and safe process that is a delicious sweet treat! Growing up, my grandma and I would bake different types of sweets and her brownies were my favorite. Doing this with my grandma, I learned and was able to make them myself, that keeping up with oven time and having a good consistency of ingredients is most important. Its something fast and simple for anyone who enjoys sweets especially brownies!

-INGREDIENTS:

1 1/2 cup sugar, 1/2 cup powdered sugar, 2/3 cup cocoa powder, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla, 2 eggs, 3/4 cup flour, 1/2 cup canola oil, 2 tablespoons water, and 3/4 teaspoons salt.

-STEPS:

Melt butter, combine with sugar, mix ingredients into a bowl, then pour evenly into an oven-safe pan. (8x8 dish) Preheat the oven to 320 degrees then bake for about 40-45 minutes! Let brownies cool completley before slicing. Adding choclate chips to your mixture before baking is a great idea to make your brownies better!

Allgeries: *All brownies contain dairy, milk, soya, eggs, and some may contain gluten.*
